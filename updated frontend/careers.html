Culinary Arts

LPA :₹3-15 Lakhs

Culinary Anthropology

Culinary Anthropology is the study of the role of food in human societies, focusing on how cultural, historical, and social factors shape dietary practices, cuisine, and the symbolic meanings of food. Culinary anthropologists analyse the ways food influences identity, social relations, religious rituals, and economics across different societies and historical periods. It’s a blend of cultural studies, history, and the culinary arts, involving research into traditional and modern food practices.

Ceramics Engineering

Ceramics Engineering is a branch of materials science and engineering that focuses on the design, creation, and application of ceramic materials. Ceramics are inorganic, non-metallic materials that are typically made from clay and other natural substances. Oenology

Oenology is the science and study of wine and winemaking.
